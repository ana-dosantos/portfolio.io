Multi-channel
B2B Campaign

Goal: Generate leads targeting aerospace companies, including MROs, OEMs, and aerospace component and parts suppliers.

Channels: Email marketing, organic social media posts, and content marketing (whitepaper).

I also created a dedicated landing page and used marketing automation tools to nurture leads. Every asset included a UTM link for tracking purposes.

Development: The campaign was aligned with both the OEM and the sales team. As distributors, we needed OEM approval.

For this specific campaign, I created a new approach designing an Infographic to tell the FPI story. I communicated the content strategy and nurture flow to the sales team, and distributed the relevant sales assets. Sales provided input on the target audience and recommended including an additional content document. While this additional case study was not fully aligned with the FPIs line, it was a strong document highlighting OEM reliability.

Results: We achieved a 22% improvement in MQLs and a 65% increase in call requests compared to the previous campaign, with approximately $1.2M in revenue attributed to the campaign.
